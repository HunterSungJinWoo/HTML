    How to make a cake

  1. Choose a Recipe
  2. Choose the Right Baking Pans
  3. Allow Ingredients to Reach Room Temperature
  4. Prep the Pans
  5. Preheat the Oven
  6. Stir Together Dry Ingredients
  7. Combine the butter and sugar
  8. Combine the Butter and Sugar
  9. Add Eggs One at a Time
  10. Alternate Adding Dry and Wet Ingredients
  11. Pour Batter Into Pans and Bake
  12. Check Cake for Doneness
  13. Cool the Cake
  14. Assemble the Cake
  15. Add the First Coat of Frosting
  16. Frost and Decorate

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened (plus extra for greasing the pans)
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 1/2 cups buttercream frosting, such as basic buttercream
